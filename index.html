Shahriar Islam Alvi (Bangla: শাহরিয়ার ইসলাম আলভী) is a Bangladeshi Entrepreneur, Cyber consultant and Journalist. He was born on 21 February in Patuakhali, Bangladesh. He is the founder and owner of Primarch Inc. which is a digital media and marketing agency.
