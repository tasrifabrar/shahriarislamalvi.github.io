Shahriar Islam Alvi (Bangla: শাহরিয়ার ইসলাম আলভী) is a Bangladeshi Entrepreneur, Cyber consultant and Journalist. He was born on 21 February in Patuakhali, Bangladesh. He is the founder and owner of Primarch Inc. which is a digital media and marketing agency.[1]

Early Life

Shahriar was a very ordinary boy in Patuakhali district of Bangladesh. But his passion do something made him something special.

He always tried to do something new. He helped the poor people of his area and also helped many people on social media who were in trouble.
